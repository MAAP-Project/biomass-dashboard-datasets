The JPL 2020 map is the global aboveground biomass (AGB) density estimates (unit: Mg/ha) derived from a combination of Landsat-8 composites, ALOS PALSAR backscatter (2019-2020) data and SRTM DEM at 100m spatial resolution, trained with samples from the spaceborne lidar data of ICESAT-1, airborne laser scanning (ALS) data, and field inventory data. We applied the boosting tree machine learning model to predict wall-to-wall AGB inferred from satellite data.

The product shared on the Multi-Mission Algorithm and Analysis Platform (MAAP) is separated continentally, including Africa, Asia, Europe, N America, Oceania, and S America. One can merge the 6 files to obtain the global mosaic.
